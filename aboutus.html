About US

BESTCLEAN started its journey in the year of 2004 with a modern plant consists of state of the art line of machineries. Evolving during these years BESTCLEAN has gained maturity in providing quality service to their valued customers. BESTCLEAN’s strive to reach out metropolitan city dwellers to serve their necessity with the help of modern technology and a touch of personal care will remain.

PICK UP

BESTCLEAN has a fleet of covered vehicles to pick up the washable clothing from its outlets through the metro city.

LAUNDRY

BESTCLEAN has a rich Washing Plant consists of modern technology based world class washing machines where international standard washing chemicals are used. There is a high quality Water Treatment Plant for uninterrupted supply of clean water into the washing plant. BESTCLEAN has a support line of high quality Dryer Machines.

DRY CLEANING

BESTCLEAN has a rich Dry Cleaning line of the latest technology based larger in size Dry Cleaning Machines from internationally famous brands. International standard cleaning chemicals are used in the whole dry cleaning process.

PRESSING

The Pressing section of BESTCLEAN consists of modern technology based high quality Pressing Machineries and enriched by a pool of highly skilled manpower in Pressing service. There is a Boiler, made in USA to supply steam into the plant without any interruption.

QUALITY CONTROL

In regards to Quality Assurance BESTCLEAN ensures Zero Tolerance.

FOLDING & PACKAGING

BESTCLEAN has a pool of highly skilled manpower in Folding and Packaging service. Wide space and High quality Polycutting Machine added values to the Folding and Packaging section.

DELIVERY

With the help of its own fleet of covered vehicles BESTCLEAN delivers washed clothing in time to the outlets.

DELIVERY TO CUSTOMER

And Finally, our valued customers collect their clothing from the respective BESTCLEAN outlet.
